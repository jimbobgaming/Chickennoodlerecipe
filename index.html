Jimbob's Chicken Noodle Soup RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Premade rotisserie chicken breast OR cooked chicken breast
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 2-3 Celery strips
  • Carrots (I get the precut ones that are shredded style)
  • Knots Chicken Cubes (pack of 6 cubes)
  • Small mini noodles (Look's like rice)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Turn stove on medium and put 6 cups of water in large soup pot and put 2tbs of salt and 3tbs of pepper then put the chicken cubes in and slightly stir and let dissolve.

  2. While the cubes dissolve dice the whole onion into small as possible pieces, then cut the 2-3 celery sticks.

  3. Once all the cutting is done put the ingredients in the pot and stir and throw in 3 handfuls of the pre-cut carrots.

  4. Put the top of the pot on and turn heat up to medium-high and let sit for 30 minutes make sure the water goes to a boil if not turn up the heat.
    Note: (While this goes you can start shredding and ripping apart the chicken make sure there is no skin on the breasts when shredding)

  5. After 30 minutes put another half cup of water in the pot and add another handful of carrots (thisâ€™ll be the rest of the carrots you use). Now let it sit for another 20 minutes with the top of pot on.

  6. After the 20 minutes is up put the pieces of chicken in and half cup of the small noodles. (You can always add more later) and turn heat to medium-low and let sit for another 20 minutes.

  7. After 10-15 minutes check up on the soup and if the noodles are absorbing too much water put 3 quarters cup of water in and let sit for 10 minutes or if not enough noodles put in more and let sit for 10 minutes.

  8. After the 5-10 minutes after checking up on it your soup is done.
